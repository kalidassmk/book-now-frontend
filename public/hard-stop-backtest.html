🛑 Hard-Stop Backtest

Replay every historical filled trade against a candidate hard-stop amount. For each trade, walks the 1m klines from fill→exit and finds the moment unrealized loss hit the stop. Shows: how many winning trades the stop would have killed (false alarm), how many losses it would have capped early, and the net P&L change.

🎚️ Stop amount + range

$0.05 $0.10 $0.15 $0.20 $0.30 $0.50
With $48/leg, $0.15 stop = -0.31% drop. Currently bot uses 1.5% hard stop = -$0.72 loss/leg. Tighter stops cap downside but kill more winners that briefly dipped before recovering.
