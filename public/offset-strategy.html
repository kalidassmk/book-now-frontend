📖 When to use each tightness level

−0.01% / −0.02% (Ultra-tight) CALM market
When 1h range < 0.5% AND coin is in a strong uptrend
Fill nearly guaranteed because price barely moves. Trade-off: less price improvement, you'll buy slightly above the very best price. Good for chasing momentum entries when you don't want to miss the boat. Watch the spread — if it's wide, tighter limit may still not fill quickly.
−0.03% / −0.05% (Tight) Calm-Normal
When 1h range 0.5–1.0% AND volume is healthy
Small but real chance the dip won't fully reach a wider limit. Balanced: better entry than ultra-tight, still likely to fill. Today's most common setting for the major pairs.
−0.15% / −0.30% (Conservative, current default)Mixed
When 1h range 1–2% (current default for CALM and NORMAL)
Designed for slightly wavy markets. Better entry price but more cancels due to drift (the RENDER/ZAMA case). Good balance for unpredictable days.
−0.70% / −1.50% (Wide) VOLATILE
When 1h range > 2% (current default for VOLATILE and X_VOLATILE)
Required for choppy coins — tighter limits miss most of the time. Trade-off: bigger drawdown if the trade goes against you. Hard stop at −1.5% keeps losses bounded.
