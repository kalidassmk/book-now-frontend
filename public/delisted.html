🚨 Pre-delisting (scheduled by Binance)

Coins Binance has officially announced for delisting on a specific date (BAPI pom: true). DUSDT on 2026-06-19 is the canonical example. We block these from every algorithm.

Coins Binance flagged as under monitoring — usually a precursor to full delisting. We block these from every scalper / algorithm.

⏸️ Status: BREAK

Pairs Binance has paused — not actively traded (exchangeInfo.status = BREAK). Sending an order to one of these would always fail.
