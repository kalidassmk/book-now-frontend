About this view: All metrics derive from Kite's /quote snapshot. Pressure is the book imbalance between pending bids and asks at any given moment — a leading indicator but not the same as executed taker volume. VWAP gap compares LTP to the session volume-weighted average; positive = buyers paying premium. Day range shows where LTP sits between the day's low (left) and high (right), with the yellow VWAP tick as a reference. A tick-level taker-flow scalper would give cleaner signal but requires the historical-data subscription on the Kite app.
